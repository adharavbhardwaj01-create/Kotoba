## 1. JLPT Vocabulary Lists (N5-N1)

  - N5: 662 | N4: 632 | N3: 1,784 | N2: 1,793 | N1: 3,463

- **Schema**: Documented, versioned, validated in CI
- **Data Fields**: word, reading, meanings, level, examples (ja/en)
- **Why Best**: Properly licensed, all formats, example sentences, maintained, CI-validated

## 7. Free Spaced Repetition Data/Formulas

### 7.1 SM-2 Algorithm (Classic)
- **Source**: Piotr Wozniak's SuperMemo 2 (1987)
- **Formula**:
  ```
  EF' = EF + (0.1 - (5-q) * (0.08 + (5-q) * 0.02))
  where:
    q = quality of response (0-5)
    EF = easiness factor (default 2.5)
    interval = previous_interval * EF
  ```
- **License**: Public domain
- **Used by**: Anki, many SRS apps
- **Quality**: ★★★★ Good — battle-tested, simple

### 7.2 FSRS (Free Spaced Repetition Scheduler)
- **Source**: https://github.com/open-spaced-repetition/fsrs4anki
- **License**: MIT
- **Quality**: ★★★★★ Excellent — modern, ML-optimized
- **Features**:
  - Machine learning optimized parameters
  - Better retention than SM-2
  - Used by Anki (newer versions)
- **npm**: `fsrs` package available
- **Parameters**: Pre-trained on large Anki datasets

### 7.3 Anki's Implementation
- **Source**: https://github.com/ankitects/anki
- **License**: AGPL-3.0
- **Quality**: ★★★★★ Excellent — gold standard
- **Note**: Open source, can study implementation
