# Research Brief: Free Resources for Kotoba Japanese Learning App

## Question
What free resources, APIs, datasets, and tools can Kotoba use to provide a comprehensive Japanese learning experience without paid dependencies?

## Scope
- Free APIs for Japanese language (translation, dictionary, kanji lookup)
- Free audio/pronunciation resources (TTS, speech synthesis)
- Free kanji/kana datasets (stroke order, readings, meanings)
- Free JLPT vocabulary/grammar data
- Free speech recognition options
- Free images/assets for Japanese cultural content
- Free hosting/deployment options beyond Vercel

## Out of scope
- Paid APIs or premium tiers
- Resources requiring enterprise licenses
- Self-hosted solutions requiring server maintenance

## Assumptions
- App is open source, BYOK model (users bring their own API keys)
- Primary AI backend is Gorp API (free)
- Need supplementary free data for vocabulary, kanji, grammar
- Target: Japanese beginners to intermediate learners

## Depth
Standard (3-5 sub-agents, 15+ sources)

## Date
2026-07-13

## Angles
1. Free Japanese dictionary/translation APIs (Jisho, JMdict, etc.)
2. Free kanji data sources (KANJIDIC, stroke order data)
3. Free JLPT vocabulary lists and grammar resources
4. Free TTS/speech synthesis for Japanese (Web Speech API alternatives)
5. Free speech recognition for Japanese
